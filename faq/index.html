The basics

What is Friendly Trader?

Friendly Trader is a suite of plain-English trading tools. They take noisy charts and confusing options and turn them into clear, explainable reads you can act on. One membership unlocks all of them.

What tools do I get?

Three are live today: The Screener (daily setups graded A to D), Friendly Options (pick cleaner contracts, analyze a trade, and read the options flow in plain English), and the Discord Trade Tracker (follow live trade alerts as they post). Two more are on the way and will be included: Friendly Intelligencesoon and BTC Edgesoon. You also get community access.

How much does it cost?

$79 per month. One membership unlocks every tool, and you can cancel anytime.

Do I need to be an experienced trader?

No. Every tool is built to read in plain English. That is the whole point: clarity over noise.

Is this financial advice?

No. These are educational tools to help you do your own research and make your own decisions. Markets involve risk.

Getting in and logging in

How do I get access?

Click Unlock Tools, check out on Whop for $79 a month, and you are a member. Then open any tool and sign in with Continue with Whop.

How do I log in to each tool?

Every tool uses the same Whop login. Your Whop account is your key: open a tool, click Continue with Whop, and you are in. There are no separate passwords to remember.

Do I log in separately for each tool?

Each tool has its own sign-in button, but it is always the same Whop account. No new account, no new password, just Continue with Whop the first time you open each one.

I am a member but a tool says I am not subscribed. What now?

Make sure you are signing in with the same Whop account you joined with. Access is checked against your Whop membership, so a different account or an email typo is the usual cause. If it keeps happening, reach out in the community and we will sort it.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep access while your membership is active. The tools re-check your membership regularly, so access ends shortly after a membership lapses. There is no contract, cancel whenever.

Already in the community

I am already in the Stocks With Josh community. Do I get the tools?

Yes. If your membership is active, the tools are already included. Just open a tool and sign in with Continue with Whop using your existing account. There is nothing extra to buy.

Then who is the $79 tier for?

The $79 Friendly Trading Tools membership is the tools on their own. It is a lower-cost way in for people who want the tools without the full community membership.

What is the difference between the tools tier and my membership?

A full membership includes the education and the live trade alerts from real traders. The $79 tools tier includes the tools plus community chat, but not the live alerts or education. Those stay with the higher tiers.
